Family Time at Togo

Here’s a picture of my family enjoying time together in Togo, taken from the highest mountain in the country, Mount Agou. Family moments are precious and close to my heart. Being surrounded by loved ones in such a breathtaking and meaningful place fills me with joy and gratitude. The kids are so happy about the trip and can’t wait to go back soon—it helps them see where they originally come from and understand their roots. These gatherings are a time to celebrate our traditions, share stories, and create memories that will last a lifetime. No matter where life takes us, the bonds we strengthen during these moments remind me of the importance of family, love, and belonging.
